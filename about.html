About

This project was made by three students from the University of Minho. The Integrated Project subject, which is part of the Master's degree in Computer Engineering, asked the students to choose a proposal (provided by the teachers) from one of the several fields of this master. We chose this specific project in the Computer Graphics field. Its aim was to provide a better understanding of the VXGI API, constructed by NVidia, due to the fact there was little information on the web about it.
