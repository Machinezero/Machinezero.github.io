What is VXGI?

Voxel Global Illumination, VXGI, is a new way, developed by NVIDIA, to simulate Global Illumination. This implementation converts the 3D scene into voxels, making them emitting or reflecting the light.

How VXGI works?

VXGI works by following some steps, like: Opacity Voxelization, Emittance Voxelization and Cone Tracing. Here you can observe a detail explanation to understand the implementation.
